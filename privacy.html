We are committed to complying with the Data Protection Act 2017, which governs how personal data is collected, processed, and stored. This policy is regularly reviewed to reflect changes in our practices, legal obligations, and advancements in technology. We recommend you revisit this page periodically to stay informed about updates. The latest version of this policy will govern our use of your information.

  • Information You Provide:

    • Responses provided in surveys, promotions, or feedback requests.
  • Transaction Details:

    • Information required for payment processing, such as account or billing details.
  • Website and Technical Data:

Elevate Global Markets does not collect sensitive personal information unless specifically required for regulatory compliance.

Elevate Global Markets uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to:

  • Distinguish you from other users on our website.

Cookies help us collect aggregated data for analytics, enabling us to optimize our website. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings. For more information, please see our Cookie Policy.

  • For Communication:

  • For Marketing:
    • Share details about new products, promotions, and features (only if you have opted-in to receive such communications).
  • To Ensure Compliance and Security:
    • Verify your identity and prevent fraud or unauthorized access.

We may disclose your personal information in specific situations, including:

  • To Service Providers:

  • To Affiliates and Successors:

Elevate Global Markets ensures that all third parties adhere to strict confidentiality and data protection requirements before sharing your information.

As a Elevate Global Markets user, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details provided in Section 10. Verification of your identity may be required before processing your request.

Elevate Global Markets employs robust security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, misuse, or loss. These measures include:

Elevate Global Markets retains personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes. Once retention is no longer necessary, we securely delete or anonymize your data.
