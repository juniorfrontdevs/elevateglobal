Cookies Policy

Welcome to Luxeracapital’s Cookie Policy ("Policy").

This Policy outlines how cookies are used on our website, www.luxeracapitaloworld.com (the "Website"). By accessing the Website, you agree to the use of cookies as described herein.

We are dedicated to improving this Policy over time. If significant updates are made, we will notify users through the Website or, where practical, via email.

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. These files allow us to recognize your device and remember your preferences or previous interactions. For the purposes of this Policy, "cookies" also include other technologies that serve similar functions, such as mobile device identifiers.

Elevate Global Markets uses cookies to enhance your experience, streamline account management, improve website performance, and assist with analytics and marketing.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies on the Website to:

  • Recognize you and provide quicker access to your account.
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent activities.
  • Save your preferences and ensure the Website works as intended.
  • Deliver a version of the Website tailored to your location or device.
  • Support marketing efforts by offering relevant services and promotions.
  • Conduct data analysis to improve the Website and better understand user behavior.

To learn more about cookies and how they work, visit www.aboutcookies.org or www.allaboutcookies.org.

3. Types of Cookies

By duration:

  • Session Cookies:Temporary cookies that expire once you close your browser. These allow us to link your activities during a single session.
  • Persistent Cookies:Stored on your device until deleted manually or until their expiration date. These retain your preferences between sessions.

By origin:

  • First-Party Cookies:Created and stored directly by the Elevate Global Markets domain to support essential Website functions.
  • Third-Party Cookies:Managed by external providers to deliver additional services, such as analytics or advertising.

By purpose:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies:These cookies are essential for the basic functionality of the Website, such as fraud prevention and secure login. Without them, certain services may not function properly.
  • Functional Cookies:Improve user experience by enabling features such as personalized settings. Disabling these may reduce the functionality of the Website.
  • Performance Cookies:Collect anonymous information to measure Website traffic and performance, helping us improve content and features.
  • Targeting Cookies:Serve advertisements tailored to your interests and measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.

Details about the purpose and lifespan of cookies used on the Website are available in our cookie management tool.

4. Third-Party Cookies

Our website may link to third-party services or applications, some of which may use cookies or similar technologies. These cookies, managed by external providers, may track analytics, performance, or advertising. Elevate Global Markets does not control third-party cookies; refer to the respective provider’s cookie policies for more details.

5. Managing Your Cookies

You have several options to manage cookies:

  • Using the Cookie Consent Tool:Adjust your cookie preferences using the "Manage Cookies" link available in the footer of the Website. This tool allows you to enable or disable specific categories of cookies, except for strictly necessary cookies.
  • Browser Settings:Change your browser settings to block or delete cookies. Check the "Help" or "Settings" menu in your browser for detailed instructions. Note that blocking cookies may impact your experience on the Website.

6. Questions and Your Rights

If you have questions about this Policy or wish to exercise your data protection rights, please refer to the Privacy Policy in the "Terms and Policies" section of our Website.
